Our Story

A Table Set in 2000

Zaiqah was born from a single belief — that Arabic food, prepared with honesty and love, is among the world's greatest cuisines. Our founders arrived in London in 2000 carrying recipes, spices and an unshakeable conviction.

Twenty-six years on, our za'atar is still stone-ground, our bread still hand-baked before every service, and every guest still leaves having felt something they did not expect: that a meal can feel like a homecoming.
